Building Cities That Create Opportunity Without Selling Off Land

We're designing a new kind of city — walkable, rooted in culture, and powered by purpose. It starts in Zanzibar, but it's meant for the world.

Kisiwa Mpya Overview

Kisiwa Mpya means "New Island." It's our 1-square-mile prototype in Zanzibar — built around clean infrastructure, tourism, and dignity. The land stays public. The people benefit. The city grows as needed, not faster.

Step Inside the Vision Why Zanzibar

Our Mission

We build cities that create jobs, housing, and clean infrastructure without selling off land or importing foreign sprawl. Everything is leased, not sold, so the government retains control and local people benefit.

Grounded Approach

No hype, no buzzwords. Just real cities for real futures, designed with and for local communities.

Shared Prosperity

Our lease model ensures economic benefits flow to local communities and government.

Sustainable Vision

1‑square‑mile prototypes focused on walkability, tourism, sustainability, and culture.
